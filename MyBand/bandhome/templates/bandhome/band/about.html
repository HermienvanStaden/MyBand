Learn more about Feral

We are a hardcore punk band based in Johannesburg, South Africa. The band was founded by brother and sister team Adam and Lisa Palaszczuk. Marilyn and Ben has been part of the line-up almost from the start.

We all bring different influences to the band: Lisa loves metal, the screechier the better. Ben has a soft spot for skate punk, but also sees the value of thrash metal. Marilyn loves classic punk and her guilty pleasure is arena rock. Just for the drumming, she says... Adam's taste is trapped in hardcore punk as last heard in LA in the 80s.

Members
  • Lisa Palaszczuk: Lead vocals, guitar
  • Adam Palaszczuk: Lead vocals, guitar
  • Marilyn McGregor: Drums
  • Ben Gaju: Base
